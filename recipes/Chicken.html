Grilled Asian Chicken

Home

A delicious and easy-to-make grilled chicken recipe with Asian-inspired flavors. The chicken is marinated in a blend of soy sauce, ginger, garlic, and sesame oil, then grilled to perfection. This recipe is perfect for a quick weeknight dinner or weekend barbecue.

Ingredients:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons sesame oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon fresh ginger, grated
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 1 tablespoon rice vinegar
  • Green onions for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, combine soy sauce, sesame oil, garlic, ginger, honey, and rice vinegar.
  2. Place chicken in a large zip-lock bag and pour marinade over it.
  3. Marinate in refrigerator for at least 2 hours or overnight.
  4. Preheat grill to medium-high heat.
  5. Grill chicken for 6-7 minutes per side, or until internal temperature reaches 165Â°F.
  6. Let rest for 5 minutes before serving.
  7. Garnish with chopped green onions.
