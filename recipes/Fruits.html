Ingredients:

  • 2 cups sweet potatoes, cubed
  • 1 bunch fresh kale, chopped
  • 1/2 cup red onion, diced
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons apple cider vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C)
  2. Toss sweet potatoes with half of the olive oil
  3. Roast sweet potatoes for 25-30 minutes until tender
  4. Mix remaining olive oil with vinegar and honey
  5. Combine roasted potatoes, kale, and onion in a bowl
  6. Pour dressing over salad and toss
  7. Season with salt and pepper
